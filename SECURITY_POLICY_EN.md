# Security and Privacy Policy

**Last updated: December 16, 2025**

TomFinder was developed with a complete focus on privacy, transparency, and security. This Policy describes how information is handled during the use of the application.

**Developer:** Alesson Viana  
**Country:** Canada  
**Platform:** Android  
**Contact Email:** alesson.viana@gmail.com

## 1. Information Collection

TomFinder does not collect, store, or share users' personal data.

Specifically:

- No account creation or authentication is required
- No name, email, phone number, or personal identifier is requested
- No user profiles are created
- No location data is collected
- The application can be used entirely without providing any personal data

## 2. Audio Usage

TomFinder allows users to:

- Use a piano pad to sing or vocalize
- Discover the most suitable musical key (note) for a song

The captured audio is used exclusively for real-time analysis, with the sole purpose of identifying the musical key.

We guarantee that:

- Audio is not recorded, stored, or reused
- No recording is linked to a user
- Audio is not used for behavioral analysis, marketing, or profiling
- Processing occurs temporarily and securely

## 3. Use of Artificial Intelligence

TomFinder uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) with technology based on the Gemini LLM, only for functional audio analysis and musical key detection.

We clarify that:

- AI does not generate content, texts, or automated decisions
- No personal data is sent or stored by AI services
- Audio is not used for model training
- There is no profiling, behavioral prediction, or automated decision-making
- AI is used exclusively as a technical tool for musical analysis

## 4. Advertising

TomFinder is a free application and may display advertisements to support its maintenance.

We use the Google AdMob platform, which may collect non-personal and aggregated data, such as:

- Device type
- Ad interactions
- Ad performance metrics

This data is handled according to Google's Privacy Policy. TomFinder does not access, control, or store this information.

## 5. Donations

If the application offers a donation option:

- Payments are processed exclusively by third-party platforms (such as Google Play)
- TomFinder does not collect, process, or store financial data or payment information

## 7. Data Sharing

Since TomFinder does not collect personal data:

- No information is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties
- There is no commercialization or misuse of data

## 8. Children's Privacy

TomFinder does not collect personal data from children under 13 years of age (or the minimum age defined by local law).

Since no personal data is collected, the application is considered suitable for general audiences.

## 9. Information Security

Since there is no storage of personal or sensitive data:

- Risks related to data breaches are minimized
- All temporary processing follows information security best practices

## 10. Changes to this Policy

This Privacy and Security Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be communicated through the application or its store page, with an update to the revision date.
